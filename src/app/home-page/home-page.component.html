About Communica

Communica is Project Management Application designed by Team Impulse, India. The idea was to create a single stop solution to all your Project Management worries. You can communicate within the team, share your progress, add new projects, assign task to your team-mates with the help of Communica, which is a web-based application, so no need to even download it!!

In App Features

  • Streamline your project management process.
  • Meet deadlines faster with project calender.
  • Collababroate with multiple teams easily.
  • Create task dependency and save time.
  • Share files, discuss and comment on projects.
